Testing out cos & sin on circle

You can use the Math.cos and Math.sin methods to find the coordinates of a certain point on a circles edge. Based on the horizontal radius, You can find the horizontal or vertical distance that a line drawn from the center of a circle would hit the edge of the circle while drawn at a certain angle(defined in radians) from the horizontal radius.

Rotate and Translate

Rotation are essential for canvas web designs to rotate items along around a predefined axis. Translating moves the canvas to position specified by the x and y coordinates. Uncontrolled canvas drawings such as the first are caused by using the same canvas instance for every rotation. The .save() and .restore() methods are helpful in ensuring the canvas context instance in use is refreshed after each rotation or translation, or repeated iteration of both.
